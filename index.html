THE ENGINE BEHIND THE NUMBERS

A fully reproducible geospatial pipeline.

6 Landsat epochs analyzed
2000–2024 Observation window
5 Mitigation policies simulated
CA-Markov Forecasting kernel

Rasterio and GeoPandas drive the spatial processing layer. Hargreaves-Samani computes evaporation from PMD temperature and extraterrestrial radiation. NDWI and NDBI indices are calibrated per Landsat generation to isolate water and built-up pixels. A logistic growth curve is fit against 25 years of observed sprawl, then a CA-Markov kernel propagates urbanization forward on a 30m grid until the storage volume crosses the dead-storage threshold.
