Policy Simulator

Adjust the four levers below to test custom mitigation combinations. The right-side outputs update live using the same sediment-accounting equations from the Python pipeline.

Sediment Trap Efficiency 0%

Check dam cascades intercept sediment before it reaches the reservoir. Scenario B uses 25%.

Afforestation Reclaim Rate 0 ac/yr

Annual acres of watershed land returned to forest. Reduces the effective transformed area driving sediment generation. Scenario D uses 300 ac/yr.

Urban Growth Restraint 0%

Spatial zoning aggressiveness. 0% = unrestricted sprawl (Scenario A). 100% = complete containment (theoretical ceiling). Scenario C sits at approximately 70%.

Climate Stress +5%

Deviation from baseline precipitation. Wetter climates accelerate erosion and sediment transport. Baseline forecasts assume +5%.

Runs 1,000 Monte Carlo simulations with jittered parameters (±10% sediment rate, ±8% growth rate, ±5% climate, ±3pp trap efficiency) and displays the 90% confidence envelope around the median trajectory. Uncheck for single-run deterministic view.

Validation note

The simulator replicates the sediment-decline math from Engines 4 and 6 of the pipeline. It does not re-run the CA-Markov spatial simulation, so custom scenarios share Scenario A's logistic sprawl curve by default, modified by the zoning restraint slider.
