Reading the Map

The teal dashed line marks the full catchment — every drop of rain falling inside it drains to the lake. Urban pixels come from Landsat NDBI at 30-metre resolution, differenced against the year 2000 baseline. The scenario radios overlay one of the five CA-Markov forecasts for 2088; afforestation scenarios (D, E) also display the reclaimed-land patches in lime green.
